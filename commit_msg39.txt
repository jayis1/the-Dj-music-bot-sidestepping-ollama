Station Wars: Complete Implementation Summary

Core Concept
Station Wars is an inter-dimensional frequency bleed system. Instead of a normal commercial, a radio station from another dimension/kosmos bleeds onto the frequency for ~15 seconds. The hijack voices are the same 3 commercial voices (am_adam, bf_emma, bm_george) — but they're from another kosmos, so the content is alien and wrong, not the voice. After the bleed, the DJ cuts back in with a recovery line, then the normal DJ intro plays, then the song.

Flow
play_next() → should_play_hijack() (5% chance, checked FIRST)
  → HIJACK: → generate_hijack() → _dj_speak(hijack, voice=random_commercial_voice)
              → _hijack_pending_recovery[guild_id] = True
              → _on_tts_done → _play_song_after_dj()
              → detects _hijack_pending_recovery → DJ recovery line
              → sets _commercial_pending_intro → DJ intro → song
  → NO HIJACK: → should_play_commercial() (15% chance, normal flow)
